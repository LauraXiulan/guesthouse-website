Attractions

Sintra

Sintra is a small town located half an hour from Ericeira. It is full of colorful and impresive castles and palaces with Palácio da Pena being the most noticable one.

Lisbon

Lisbon the capital city of Portugal is only 45 minutes away from Ericeria. Enjoy the history that the city has to offer, eat pastéis de nata de Belém or visit a Fado performance.

Cabo da Roca

Cabo da Roca is the wesernmost point on the European continent. The cape has a beautiful dramatic scenery with high cliffs overlooking the ocecan. Less than an hour away
