Welcome

Ola Onda Guesthouse is located in the beautiful fisherman village Ericeira in South-Portugal. We welcome all guests to stay in our comfortable rooms or book a private appartment to enjoy with your family or friends. We are located both near the beach and the village center, so you can either walk or rent a bike with us. Explore Ericeira and the surrounding area with our pre-arranged tours or enjoy surfing, Port tastings, Yoga or any other activities on offer.
